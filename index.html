World Map

Use the Left and Right arrow keys to slide the map west or east; Page Up and Page Down slide it by a larger step; Home re-centers it. Tab to the red or blue point to move the points.

Use the arrow keys to move this point by one degree: Left and Right change longitude, Up and Down change latitude. Page Up and Page Down change longitude by fifteen degrees; Home and End jump to the prime meridian and the date line. The snap and restrict options apply. The other point always stays on the opposite side of the Earth.

Transparent Globe View

A transparent globe showing the same two antipodal points. Drag the globe to rotate it, or use the arrow keys when the globe itself is focused. Tab to the red or blue point to move it with the arrow keys.
